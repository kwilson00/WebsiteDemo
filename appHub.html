How To Use The App

  1. Download SiteSnap off the app store.
  2. After installation, load up SiteSnap.
  3. Click or wait for the splash screen to end.
  4. Log into an existing account or sign up to SiteSnap.
  5. Click New Evidence or View Evidence to manage or store evidence.
  6. If creating new evidence - Use appropriate and memorable titles and descriptions.
  7. Share the evidence by clicking the share icon.
  8. You can download your evidence by clicking the download button (after clicking share).

The app helps students stay organised and provides quick access to important coursework and information regarding the work. The app also helps student easily store, manage and share their past coursework to ensure they do not lose it.
